# Study Resources

Short, bite-sized references to pair with your Art Summaries + Lessons 1–2.  
Treat this like a little menu – you only need **one link per session**.

---

## How to Use This File

- **Session length:** 10–20 minutes max.
- **Pattern:**  
  1. Skim the matching section in your **Art Summaries**.  
  2. Watch/read **one** resource below.  
  3. Add 1–2 lines of notes to your summaries or your changelog.
