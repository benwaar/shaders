## VALUE DESIGN

Use a clear three-step value hierarchy so the image reads in grayscale:
- Lightest: orb and its immediate glow are the brightest elements in the image.
- Darkest: cassowary body and head are kept as a single clearly darker value than the background for a strong silhouette.
- Mid-tones: HUD circles, tech shapes, and background gradients sit in middle values that support the silhouette and never compete with the orb.

No other area should be as bright as the orb, and background details should stay low-contrast so the focal point remains obvious even in black and white.
